Basic info

I am Felipe Reyes, a former PhD student in the Geometry and Mathematical Physics program in SISSA.

Research Interest

I am interested in properties of Dubrovin-Frobenius manifolds around their caustic. These manifolds where invented by Boris Dubrovin to geometrize the study of the WDVV equations. These manifolds also have connections with singularity theory and integrable systems which are also part of my interests.
